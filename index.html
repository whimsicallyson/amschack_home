Meeting your high-temperature and special-design heat transfer needs since 1956

ALSTOM Power Inc., Energy Recovery designs and manufactures high temperature and special design heat transfer equipment for some of industry's toughest environments - corrosives, erosive particulates, high pressures, and reducing atmospheres. Applications include chemical, petroleum, primary metals, and incineration. Units supplied feature state-of-the-art engineering and custom design to suit individual installation requirements.
